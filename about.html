About Pew Pew

Hello! My name is Dias Iskrayev, the creator of 'Pew Pew' project! This game was developed using JavaScript and Three.js library. The rules are simple. Press T to turn the timer ON and take down all 5 targets as fast as you can! My personal record is 02.71 seconds, can you beat it? Let's see! Hint: there is no collisions in the game, so bullets can travel throught the walls. And yes, don't fall of the map! Checkout GitHub for this project.
